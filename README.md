# JobFit Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

## Data Collection
JobFit does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data 
on external servers.

## Local Storage
Your CV text is stored exclusively in your browser's local 
storage (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your 
device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

## LinkedIn Data
JobFit reads job posting content from LinkedIn pages solely 
to perform local analysis. This data is processed in your 
browser and is never transmitted anywhere.

## Third Parties
JobFit does not share any data with third parties. 
No analytics, no tracking, no advertising.
